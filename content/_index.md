## About

<br />

 Malte Dahlberg's compositional practice is oriented towards chimeric listening, confusion and deliberately abstruse interactive systems, but also performative practices, collaborative processes, teaching and organising. He studied philosophy, architecture and composition in Gothenburg, Umeå and Stockholm. He has been the chair of the arts society and artist-run venue [Fylkingen](https://fylkingen.se/en) since 2024.
