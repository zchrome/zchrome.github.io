## About

<br />

 Malte Dahlberg is a composer living in Stockholm. He works with chimeric listening, confusion and deliberately abstruse interactive systems, but also performative practices, collaborative processes, teaching and organising. He has been the chair of the arts society and artist-run venue [Fylkingen](https://fylkingen.se/en) since 2024.
